New Students

Here are a few tips for your first class:
  • All begining students who are new to yoga should start with Level 1 or Basics classes.
  • Unless otherwise noted, all of the classes on our regular class schedule are on-going. That means there is no need to pre-register.
  • Bring a water bottle and small hand towel. If you have a yoga mat please bring it. If not, we have studio mats you can use.
  • Come 5-15 minutes early for your classes, so you can take your time to settle in.
  • Wear comfortable clothes such as sweats, leggings, t-shirts, tank tops. No shoes will be needed.
